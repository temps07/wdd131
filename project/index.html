Sweet & Savory: Easy Recipes for Busy Students

This name was chosen because it reflects the site's focus on simple, delicious recipes that cater to students and busy individuals. The combination of "sweet" and "savory" highlights the variety of recipes available, from desserts to main dishes. Itâ€™s catchy, easy to remember, and clearly communicates the site's purpose.

Optional domain availability: sweet-savory.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide easy-to-follow, affordable, and time-saving recipes tailored for students and busy individuals. It will serve as a go-to resource for quick meals, snacks, and desserts, along with tips for meal prep, grocery shopping, and kitchen hacks. The site will also include a blog with articles on cooking techniques, ingredient substitutions, and budget-friendly cooking ideas.

Scenarios

These scenarios represent questions that the target audience (students and busy individuals) might ask when visiting the site:

  • What are some quick and healthy breakfast ideas I can make in under 10 minutes?
  • How can I meal prep for the week without spending too much time or money?
  • What are some easy recipes for beginners with limited cooking experience?
  • Where can I find recipes that use only a few ingredients?
